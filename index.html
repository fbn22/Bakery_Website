Chimney Cake


Flóra will bake for you what she does best, and that's chimney cakes. A chimney cake is a special type of cake originally made in Hungary and its surrounding countries. It has a curious and unique shape for which it owns its name - chimney cake.

You can eat your chimney cake empty or filled with Nutella, ice cream, whipped cream, cinnamon... you get the idea :)
